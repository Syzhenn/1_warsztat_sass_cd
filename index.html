What is Codewise?

We started out in 2010 in Kraków, Poland with the goal to
create custom software for our founder, an independent
digital marketer.

Exceeding even his expectations, our products quickly
became popular among thousands of affiliate marketers
& performance advertising agencies.

Today, we pride ourselves with providing industry-leading
tools, support and education to our clients around the globe.
And we enjoy every minute of it!
